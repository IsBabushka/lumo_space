Our Story

What began as a simple assignment in an ITS121 class has grown into a global phenomenon. Tasked with creating a functional and innovative project, we envisioned a digital platform to showcase modern furniture that blends comfort, style, and sustainability. That vision became Lumo Space, a brand dedicated to transforming living spaces with thoughtfully designed pieces.

Today, Lumo Space is no longer just a class project—it’s a multi-trillion-dollar company revolutionizing the way people experience design. From our flagship Luma Series to budget-friendly essentials in the Brio Collection, every piece reflects our commitment to quality and innovation.

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

At Lumo Space, quality is non-negotiable. Each piece is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who share our passion for excellence. From hand-stitched upholstery to precision-engineered frames, no detail is overlooked.

We use only the finest materials—soft velvets, sustainable woods, and durable metals—to ensure our furniture stands the test of time. When you choose Lumo Space, you’re choosing pieces that are as functional as they are beautiful.

Your Home, Your Lumo Space

We know that choosing furniture is personal. That’s why we’re here to guide you every step of the way—from selecting the perfect piece to customizing it to fit your style.

With flexible payment options, easy assembly, and a hassle-free return policy, we’re committed to making your experience as seamless as possible. Your satisfaction is our success.
